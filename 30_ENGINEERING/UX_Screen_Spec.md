## Navigation (from Design System)

Home · Blueprint · Looks · Explore · Profile.

## Onboarding flow (screens)

1. **Welcome** — value proposition; privacy note; "Start".
2. **Face Photo** — capture/upload; framing guide; retake.
3. **Body Photo** — capture/upload; framing guide; retake.
4. **Analysing** — progress state while AI reads variables.
5. **Validation Questions** — short quiz, one idea per screen.
6. **Blueprint Ready** — reveal moment → into the Blueprint.

## Core screens

- **Home** — educational-first entry; quick links to Blueprint and Looks.
- **Blueprint** — the five-layer result; signature looks as the headline.
- **Look detail** — complete-look image, hero pieces with tiers, "why it works".
- **Explore** — browse guidance by category (body, colour, face); item verdicts (Yes / With Adjustments / No).
- **Profile** — variables summary (kept friendly), preferences, retake photos.

## Interaction principles (from brand)

Whitespace-first, generous spacing, simple hierarchy, mobile-first, luxury
editorial cards (soft borders, rounded corners, restrained shadows). Primary
button: Ink on Porcelain.

## Components

Look card · Recommendation row (with tier chip) · Verdict badge · Educational
copy block · Photo-capture frame · Quiz question card.

Sorune Brand Guidelines v1
==========================

Executive Summary\
\
Sorune is a premium personal style platform that helps women discover what genuinely suits them and build wardrobes they actually wear.\
\
Unlike traditional fashion platforms, Sorune is not focused on trends, influencer culture, or fashion theory for its own sake.\
\
Sorune exists to help women make better style decisions faster.\
\
By combining body shape analysis, colour analysis, face styling, and practical outfit guidance into a single personalised Style Blueprint, Sorune provides clarity where most style advice creates confusion.\
\
Mission\
Help women discover what genuinely suits them and build wardrobes they actually wear.\
\
Vision\
Become the world\'s most trusted personal style blueprint.\
\
Positioning\
Sorune is a premium personal style platform that translates complex style knowledge into practical, personalised recommendations.\
\
Brand Promise\
Every interaction should leave users feeling more confident, more clear, more intentional, and less overwhelmed.\
\
Brand Personality\
Clear, Refined, Intelligent, Supportive, Intentional, Calm.\
\
Voice & Tone\
Clarity over complexity. Guidance over rules. Confidence over fashion. Calm over hype.\
\
Sorune Aesthetic\
Editorial elegance, modern minimalism, timeless femininity, premium restraint, intentional design.\
\
Visual Identity Principles\
Luxury editorial feel. Premium photography. Clean design. Timeless presentation.\
\
Marketing Principles\
Confidence through clarity. Style made simpler. Better decisions, better wardrobes.\
\
Content Principles\
Teach. Clarify. Inspire.\
\
AI Principles\
Increase clarity, confidence, and personalisation while preserving consistency.\
\
Future Expansion Principles\
Style Personality, Wardrobe Planning, Shopping Assistant, Outfit Builder, Capsule Wardrobes, Beauty Blueprint, Travel Packing.\
\
Final Principle\
Sorune is helping women become more intentional, confident and efficient through better style decisions.\
\
Fashion changes.\
Confidence lasts.

## Colour Philosophy & Palette (added v2 — Decision 005)

**The brand is the gallery wall, not the painting.** Sorune analyses each user's
personal colours. The brand therefore stays a quiet, cool, near-neutral canvas so
the user's own colours are the colour — and so the identity never clashes with a
cool-toned user. (This replaces the original warm earth-tone palette, which did
not suit a colour-analysis product.)

**Palette (cool neutral, defined ladder):** Porcelain #F4F5F7 · Cloud #DDE0E5 · Pewter #9197A1 · Graphite #4A4F58 · Ink #15171B. **Signature accent (sparing):** Plum #5E4F66 (cool muted plum). **Whisper:** Mist #C7D2D7.

**Typography:** Cormorant Garamond (headings) + Inter (body).

## Design Ethos

Sorune should feel **unique, timeless, feminine and understated — and never
AI-generated or templated.** Distinction comes from restraint, confident editorial
typography and generous space, not decoration or trend-chasing. Fashion changes;
confidence lasts — and so should the look.

**Stylist App, Styling Repository**

The base
========

**Body shapes (5):**

-   Hourglass, Pear, Inverted triangle, Rectangle (includes athletic), Apple

**Face shapes (6):**

-   Oval, Round, Square, Heart, Long, Diamond

**Clothing categories (8):**

-   Tops, Sweaters & Knits , Dresses, Skirts, Shorts, Jeans & Trousers, Jackets & coats, Swimwear

Necklines do not get their own section. They are built into Tops, Knitwear and Dresses, and the recommendation blends face shape and chest, not body shape alone.

**Open decisions for you to settle in your edit:**

-   Jackets & coats: I have merged the old \"Jackets\" and \"Outerwear\" into one section to avoid two overlapping lists. Split them again if you prefer.

-   Knitwear: kept as its own section (separate from Tops). Remove it if you would rather fold knits into Tops.

-   Turtleneck and halter necklines, and bandeau swim tops, are deliberately rare. They suit narrow cases only. Keep as occasional picks or cut.

**Wording rule on every page:**

-   Lead positive: \"Your best\", \"Most flattering\". Soften the cautions: \"Tread carefully\", \"Skip\". Never the word \"worst\".
